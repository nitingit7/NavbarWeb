About

I am a student pursuing B.Sc. (Hons.) Physics at Delhi University, driven by curiosity about how the universe works—from the smallest particles to the largest cosmic structures.

Physics is not just a subject for me; it is a way of thinking. It teaches logical reasoning, problem-solving, and the courage to question the unknown.

The future of physics lies in pushing boundaries—whether through quantum technologies, space exploration, renewable energy, or fundamental research. As a physics student, I aspire to contribute to this future by learning deeply, thinking critically, and applying science for the betterment of society.

Physics today is discovery; physics tomorrow is possibility.
